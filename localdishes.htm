Çibörek

Although it is known as "çiğbörek" in general, this flavor, which is actually called "çibörek", is the most beautiful contribution of Crimean Tatars to Eskişehir. The combination of onion, mincemeat and various spices is the result of the delicious meeting of the paste with the dough. Almost every restaurant in Eskişehir offers different kinds of ribs with different materials.

We strongly recommend using it with yogurt or salted sauces.
